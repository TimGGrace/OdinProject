Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Have you ever wanted to make a lasagna with a crispy top that just melts in your mouth?
Well this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • 5 Lasagna Sheets
  • Hard white cheese
  • 1 Tin of Peeled Plum Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Oat Milk
  • Vegan mince
  • Dried Basil and Oregano
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Step by Step

Originally intended as a joke, this recipe should actually work.

  1. Make Mince Sauce (Fry mince, add tomatoes and herbs)
  2. Heat oven to 200C.
  3. Make Bechmel sauce.
  4. Layer repeatedly: Mince, Lasagna sheet, Bechmel, repeat
  5. Add cheese to the top layer, then bake for 15 minutes
